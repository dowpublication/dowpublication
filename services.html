Our Services

How can we help you?

Copyright

Its your book, and it is imperative that you own all the rights to your content to enjoy unabridged control over it. With Dead of Writes Publication, Author and Publisher contract, we make sure this happens.

Contract

This most essential right, which is completely under your name, that ensures no possible imitation. You are not required to share the income or credit with anyone if your story gets selected for something as big as a motion picture.

Editing

Beyond the shadow of a doubt, editing is the most crucial part of publication. We, at Dead of Writes, arrange highly professional editors for your book to make your content error-proof.

Pro-Design

In this cut-throat competitive world of book selling, it is necessary to have a great design for your script to avoid being passed over. Our qualified professional designers understand your script and deliver the perfect design.

Top-Notch Print Quality

Low grade pages and poor print quality can be the biggest spoil. A unique and bright book is more likely to be picked. We, at Dead of Writes, use the best in class printing machines to get you unmatched results.

Price Control

An unreasonably high priced book cannot possibly succeed in the market. We understand the importance of competitive pricing and give you the freedom of naming your own price.

Marketing Assistance

Writing and getting published is just half the battle won. Right and effective promotion certainly decides the fate of the other half, and thus, the overall victory. From national media coverage to street light vendors, we arrange every platform for your books viewership.

Transparency

It is of sheer significance that the writer is aware of each step taken to climb the book up the ladder of publication, to monitor and control the final result as desired. We abide by our commitments and do all that we promise to keep you well acquainted of the progress.

100% Profit

We know what it takes to write a book and what the author deserves. Dead of Writes is proud to be able to offer our authors one hundred percent of profit from all sales proceeds.
